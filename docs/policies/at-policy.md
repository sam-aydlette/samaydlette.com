# AT — Awareness and Training

Training is the annual review and update of the operational runbooks ([incident response](../incident-response.md), [recovery plan](../recovery-plan.md), [supply chain](../supply-chain.md), and this set of family policies). Information-spillage scenarios are covered as part of that review (NIST IR-9.2). The training record is [`training-log.md`](../training-log.md).

**20x rule integration.** Aligns with KSI-CED (Cybersecurity Education).

**Review cadence.** Annually with the [security review](../security-review.md).
