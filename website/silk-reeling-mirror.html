How it works

Movement in, instructor-style feedback out.

01

Pose, in the browser

Your webcam drives on-device pose estimation — nothing leaves your machine but the numbers.

02

Frame-by-frame compare

Your movement is aligned to a recognized master's and scored by per-joint angular deviation.

03

Feedback in Chen terms

The differences are translated into instructor-style guidance grounded in Chen terminology.

04

Spoken guidance

Stand back from the screen — the app speaks your posture corrections and counts your spirals aloud.

A 2D practice mirror — geometry, not a substitute for a teacher.
